Conference Tracks

Explore Diverse Areas of Indian Knowledge Systems

Sub - Themes

Original research papers, articles, case studies invited from researchers, scholars and industry professionals that may cover the following themes, but are not limited to these.

पुनराविष्कार - Rediscovering India's Scientific Heritage for the Future

TRACK-01

This track explores the ancient science and scientific heritage of India, focusing on their integration with modern scientific practices. We examine evidence-based approaches to traditional sciences, technological innovations from ancient texts, and the role of traditional knowledge in contemporary scientific advancement.

Traditional Indian contributions to mathematics, astronomy, metallurgy, and physical sciences
Integration of IKS with STEM education and innovations
Vedic insights in modern scientific and technological paradigms
Sanskrit as a scientific and computational language

आयुर्वेदयोगेन ऐक्यम् - Mind-Body-Soul Alignment through Ayurveda, Yoga, and Wellness Practices

TRACK-02

This track focuses on the scientific validation of Ayurvedic and yogic practices and their applications in modern wellness and healthcare. We explore the physiological, psychological, and neurological effects of traditional healing systems, along with their therapeutic applications.

Ayurvedic foundations of preventive and therapeutic care
Panchakarma, Dravyaguna, and Yogic practices for modern health challenges
Culinary wisdom and the role of nutrition in traditional Indian healing
Relevance of psychological and spiritual well-being in contemporary healthcare

पारम्परिकवास्तुनवदृष्टि - Reimagining Space through Indic Architectural Wisdom

TRACK-03

This track examines traditional Indian architectural practices and their relevance to modern sustainable design. We explore ancient building techniques, sacred geometry, Vastu principles, and climate-responsive architecture based on traditional wisdom.

Sacred geometry and vastu in traditional Indian architecture
Ancient city planning, temple architecture, and their sociocultural context
Environmentally sensitive housing and sustainable planning
Relevance of traditional construction practices in modern urbanization

धर्मनयनेतत्त्वम् - Ancient Indian Thought in Law, Public Policy and Business Leadership

TRACK-04

This track explores ancient Indian philosophical and legal systems and their relevance to contemporary governance and leadership. We examine concepts of dharma, justice, ethics, and their applications in modern policy-making, business leadership, and legal frameworks.

Chanakya Niti, Arthashastra and Indian models of statecraft and diplomacy
Vedic insights in leadership, decision-making and Corporate Social Responsibility
Indian economic thought and sustainability in global policy
Concept of Nyaya, Mitakshara, Manusmriti and other legal texts
Comparative analysis of ancient Indian and modern legal system

शब्दसंस्कृतेः संरक्षणम् - Preserving India's Linguistic and Literary Heritage

TRACK-05

This track celebrates the rich linguistic and literary heritage of India. We explore the preservation and revitalization of ancient languages, the role of literature in knowledge transmission, and the cognitive aspects of multilingualism in Indian traditions.

Sanskrit, Prakrit, Pali and regional languages as carriers of IKS
Language and cognition in Indian philosophy
Revitalizing endangered languages for cultural sustainability
Role of literature and oral traditions in transmitting knowledge

पारम्पर्यवेद्यं जीवनविन्यासम् - Nature in Harmony: Traditional Knowledge for Sustainable Living

TRACK-06

This track examines traditional Indian ecological knowledge and sustainable living practices. We explore ancient environmental wisdom, traditional agriculture, water conservation systems, and community-based natural resource management.

Jal Shakti: Traditional water conservation systems
Indigenous agricultural practices and food security
Festivals, ecology and the agrarian calendar
Role of women and community in environmental stewardship
