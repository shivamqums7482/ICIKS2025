Conference Tracks

Explore the diverse areas of Indian Knowledge Systems

Six Comprehensive Tracks

Our conference features six carefully curated tracks that explore different aspects of Indian Knowledge Systems, from ancient scientific traditions to contemporary applications.

पुनराविष्कार - Rediscovering India's Scientific Heritage for the Future

Track 1

This track focuses on rediscovering and revitalizing India's rich scientific heritage, exploring how ancient knowledge systems can contribute to modern scientific advancement and innovation.

Sub-themes include:

Ancient Mathematics & Astronomy

Exploring contributions of ancient Indian mathematicians and astronomers to modern science

Traditional Chemistry & Metallurgy

Understanding ancient Indian knowledge of chemistry, metallurgy, and material science

Agricultural Sciences

Traditional farming practices, crop rotation, and sustainable agriculture methods

Engineering & Technology

Ancient Indian engineering marvels and their relevance to modern technology

आयुर्वेदयोगेन ऐक्यम् - Mind-Body-Soul Alignment through Ayurveda, Yoga, and Wellness Practices

Track 2

This track explores the holistic approach to health and wellness through Ayurveda, Yoga, and other traditional Indian healing practices, emphasizing the integration of mind, body, and soul.

Sub-themes include:

Ayurvedic Medicine

Traditional diagnostic methods, herbal remedies, and personalized treatment approaches

Yoga & Meditation

Ancient practices for physical, mental, and spiritual well-being

Wellness Practices

Traditional therapies, lifestyle practices, and preventive healthcare

Mental Health

Ancient approaches to psychological well-being and emotional balance

पारम्परिकवास्तुनवदृष्टि - Reimagining Space through Indic Architectural Wisdom

Track 3

This track examines traditional Indian architectural principles, Vastu Shastra, and design philosophies, exploring their application in contemporary urban planning and sustainable architecture.

Sub-themes include:

Vastu Shastra

Ancient principles of space design and their scientific basis

Urban Planning

Traditional city planning concepts and their modern applications

Sustainable Architecture

Eco-friendly building practices and climate-responsive design

Art & Aesthetics

Traditional Indian art forms and their integration in modern design

धर्मनयनेतत्त्वम् - Ancient Indian Thought in Law, Public Policy and Business Leadership

Track 4

This track explores the application of ancient Indian philosophical and ethical principles in contemporary governance, legal systems, and business leadership practices.

Sub-themes include:

Legal Philosophy

Ancient Indian jurisprudence and its relevance to modern legal systems

Governance & Policy

Traditional governance models and their application in public administration

Business Ethics

Ancient principles of ethical business practices and corporate responsibility

Leadership Philosophy

Traditional concepts of leadership and their modern applications

शब्दसंस्कृतेः संरक्षणम् - Preserving India's Linguistic and Literary Heritage

Track 5

This track focuses on the preservation, promotion, and contemporary relevance of India's diverse linguistic traditions and literary heritage, including Sanskrit and regional languages.

Sub-themes include:

Sanskrit Studies

Classical Sanskrit literature, grammar, and its contemporary applications

Regional Languages

Preservation and promotion of India's diverse regional linguistic traditions

Ancient Texts

Study and interpretation of classical Indian literature and manuscripts

Literary Traditions

Oral traditions, storytelling, and their role in cultural preservation

पारम्पर्यवेद्यं जीवनविन्यासम् - Nature in Harmony: Traditional Knowledge for Sustainable Living

Track 6

This track explores traditional Indian approaches to environmental conservation, sustainable living practices, and the harmonious relationship between humans and nature.

Sub-themes include:

Environmental Conservation

Traditional practices for environmental protection and biodiversity conservation

Water Management

Ancient water harvesting techniques and sustainable water management systems

Renewable Energy

Traditional knowledge of natural energy sources and their modern applications

Sustainable Lifestyle

Traditional practices for sustainable living and minimal environmental impact

Leading the way in integrating traditional knowledge with modern education and research methodologies.
