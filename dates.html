Conference Days

Two-day international conference featuring keynote speeches, paper presentations, panel discussions, workshops, and cultural programs.

Day 2 - November 01

Final Sessions, Awards Ceremony, Closing

Time Zone Information

All dates and times are in Indian Standard Time (IST, UTC+5:30). International participants should adjust their schedules accordingly.

Notification Methods

All important updates and notifications will be sent via email to registered participants. Please ensure your email address is current.

Important Notes

Deadlines are firm and extensions will only be granted in exceptional circumstances. Late submissions may not be considered for the conference.
