About the Conference

Bridging Ancient Wisdom to Global Sustainability

Conference Overview

The Conference shall be uniquely organised in the traditional Indian manner, celeberating the Nation's rich cultural heritage. Emphasis shall be on creating an immersive atmosphere that honours India's timeless wisdom and cultural ethos. Everything from wearing traditional Indian attire, enjoying authentic Indian vegeterian cuisine, to following ancient practices such as lamp lighting, veduc chants, and traditional welcome ceremonies to mark the begining of each session shall bring a unique experience for every participant.

Also, avail the opportunity to visit the holy cities of Haridwar and Rishikesh and be a part of the devtional vibrance on the banks of river Ganga.

Conference Objectives

Knowledge Integration

To facilitate the integration of traditional Indian knowledge systems with modern scientific methodologies, creating innovative approaches to research and application.

Global Collaboration

To foster international collaboration among researchers, scholars, and practitioners working in various domains of Indian Knowledge Systems.

Sustainable Solutions

To explore how traditional knowledge can contribute to sustainable development goals and environmental conservation efforts.

Educational Innovation

To develop innovative educational frameworks that incorporate traditional wisdom into contemporary learning systems.

Conference Themes

Health & Wellness

Exploring Ayurveda, Yoga, and traditional healing practices for modern healthcare solutions and preventive medicine approaches.

Environmental Sustainability

Traditional ecological knowledge, sustainable agriculture, and ancient practices for environmental conservation and climate resilience.

Philosophy & Consciousness

Ancient philosophical systems, consciousness studies, and their relevance to modern psychology and cognitive sciences.

Science & Technology

Mathematical concepts, astronomical knowledge, and technological innovations from ancient Indian traditions.

Arts & Culture

Traditional arts, music, dance, and cultural practices as repositories of knowledge and wisdom.

Governance & Ethics

Ancient principles of governance, justice, and ethical frameworks for modern policy-making and social organization.

Expected Outcomes

  • Comprehensive research papers and publications on various aspects of Indian Knowledge Systems
  • Collaborative research initiatives between international institutions and scholars
  • Policy recommendations for integrating traditional knowledge into modern educational curricula
  • Development of innovative methodologies for validating and applying traditional knowledge
  • Creation of a global network of researchers and practitioners in Indian Knowledge Systems
  • Identification of funding opportunities for research projects in traditional knowledge domains
  • Establishment of best practices for preserving and transmitting traditional knowledge

Who can Participate?

  • Corporate Executives/Entrepreneurs/Practitioners.
  • Academicians/Research Scholars/Students.
  • Participation only through physical presence (No online participation)
