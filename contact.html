Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for the conference?

You can register online through our registration portal. Early bird discounts are available until the specified deadline. Payment can be made via credit card, bank transfer, or demand draft.

What are the paper submission guidelines?

Papers should be submitted in IEEE format with a maximum of 8 pages. All submissions undergo peer review. Detailed guidelines are available on our submission page.

Is accommodation provided?

Yes, we offer various accommodation options including on-campus guest house, partner hotels, and homestays. Advance booking is recommended due to limited availability.

How do I reach the university?

The university is well-connected by air, rail, and road. We provide detailed transportation information and can arrange pickup services for registered participants.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes, all registered participants will receive a certificate of participation. Presenters will receive additional certificates for their presentations.

Are meals included?

Conference registration includes lunch and tea breaks. Breakfast is included for on-campus accommodation. Special dietary requirements can be accommodated with advance notice.
