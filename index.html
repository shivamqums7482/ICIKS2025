About the Conference

Bridging the gap between traditional knowledge and contemporary research

The International Conference on Indian Knowledge Systems aims to explore the rich heritage of Indian traditional knowledge and its relevance in addressing contemporary global challenges. This conference brings together scholars, researchers, practitioners, and policymakers to discuss innovative approaches to integrating ancient wisdom with modern scientific methodologies.

Join us in this transformative journey as we explore how Indian Knowledge Systems can contribute to sustainable development, healthcare innovations, educational reforms, and technological advancements in the 21st century.
